Backend — routers/agency_contracts.py (yeni dosya)
Acente tarafı (admin-token + X-API-Key ile, diğer Syroce projesinden gelir):

POST /api/marketplace/v1/contracts/propose — yeni teklif
GET /api/marketplace/v1/contracts/{id} — durum sorgulama
GET /api/marketplace/v1/contracts/mine — tüm tekliflerim
DELETE /api/marketplace/v1/contracts/{id} — geri çekme
Otelci tarafı (PMS JWT):

GET /api/marketplace/incoming-requests — gelen talepler (tab: pending/active/history)
GET /api/marketplace/incoming-requests/{id} — detay
POST /api/marketplace/incoming-requests/{id}/approve — onayla (komisyon override edilebilir)
POST /api/marketplace/incoming-requests/{id}/reject — sebep ile reddet
POST /api/marketplace/incoming-requests/{id}/terminate — aktif sözleşmeyi feshet
